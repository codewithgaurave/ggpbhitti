PRINCIPAL MESSAGE

Government Polytechnic Baijpur Bhiti

Established in 2016, Government Polytechnic Baijpur Bhiti is a government-aided technical institute operating under the Department of Technical Education, Uttar Pradesh. The institute is located in Baijpur Bhiti, offering a peaceful, focused learning environment while maintaining accessibility to nearby towns.

The institution is affiliated with the Board of Technical Education, Uttar Pradesh (BTEUP) and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), ensuring adherence to national standards in technical education.

  • Shri Yogi Adityanath, Hon'ble Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh

Campus Facilities

  • Eco-friendly campus spread over 6.5 acres
  • Digital classrooms
  • Well-equipped laboratories
  • Separate hostel accommodations for boys and girls
  • On-campus mess facility
  • Green and clean learning environment

Diploma Courses Offered

  • Civil Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Textile Technology

Admissions are conducted through the Joint Entrance Examination Council Uttar Pradesh (JEECUP), a state-level entrance exam ensuring merit-based selection.

At Government Polytechnic Baijpur Bhiti, we are committed to delivering industry-relevant education and practical skills that prepare students for meaningful careers in engineering and technology.

Principal's Message

Suresh Kumar Basant

Welcome to the Government Polytechnic Baijpur Bhiti Family,

At our college, we are deeply committed to shaping the future of our students through quality education and holistic development. For years, our institution has been dedicated to nurturing young minds and empowering them to become capable, responsible, and successful individuals.

As an educator, I believe it is my duty to guide the next generation with care, wisdom, and purpose—helping them develop the skills necessary for a life of health, respect, prosperity, and fulfillment. I am passionate about seeing our students grow into confident individuals who are prepared to face challenges and contribute positively to society.

In my role as Principal, I strive to inspire our dedicated faculty, our students, and the wider community to collaborate and foster an environment that promotes academic achievement and personal well-being. I strongly advocate for continuous professional development, encouraging both teachers and leadership to embrace innovation and reflect on their practices to enhance teaching and learning outcomes.
