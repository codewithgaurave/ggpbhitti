WELCOME TO GOVERNMENT POLYTECHNIC BAIJPUR BHITI

Government Polytechnic Baijpur Bhiti was established in the year 2002 to provide quality technical education to the youth of Ambedkarnagar and surrounding regions. The institute is located in the serene surroundings of Baijpur Bhiti, offering a perfect environment for academic pursuits.

Over the years, the institute has grown significantly in terms of infrastructure and academic offerings. The campus spans over 25 acres of land with well-equipped laboratories, workshops, and a rich library. Our institute is committed to producing skilled technicians who can contribute to the nation's industrial growth. The institute offers diploma courses in Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Electrical Engineering, with plans to introduce more programs in the coming years.
