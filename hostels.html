Facilities at Glance

GOVERNMENT POLYTECHNIC BAIJPUR BHITI is dedicated to providing students with a supportive and resource-rich environment that enhances both academic and personal development.

Experienced Faculty & Learning Resources

Our College is staffed with well-qualified and committed faculty. Fully equipped laboratories and a comprehensive library support student learning.

Computer Lab

A well-furnished and updated computer lab enables students to gain essential digital and technical skills.

Engineering Workshops

Workshops outfitted with modern equipment for hands-on training in:

  • Carpentry
  • Smithy
  • Sheet Metal
  • Fitting
  • Welding
  • Plumbing
  • Painting
  • Machine Operations

Civil & Mechanical Engineering Labs

Specialized labs offer practical learning opportunities for Civil and Mechanical Engineering subjects, enhancing conceptual understanding through real-world applications.

Ventilated Classrooms

Spacious and well-ventilated classrooms create a comfortable environment conducive to learning.

Library

A rich collection of textbooks, reference materials, and journals is available for student use.

Green Campus

The eco-friendly campus includes residential quarters within a secure boundary.

Clean Drinking Water

Filtered drinking water is provided across the campus to ensure student health and hygiene.

We provide comfortable and secure hostel accommodations for both boys and girls with all necessary amenities to ensure a pleasant stay.

Girls Hostel

Secure and comfortable hostel for female students with special safety measures:

  • Dedicated female security personnel
  • Comfortable living spaces with attached bathrooms
  • Common kitchenette for snacks
  • 24/7 CCTV surveillance
  • Nutritious vegetarian meals
  • Visitors' lounge for guests
  • Regular health check-ups

Hostel Rules & Regulations

To ensure safety and discipline, all hostel residents must follow:

  • Strict curfew timings
  • Visitor registration process
  • No smoking/alcohol policy
  • Room maintenance guidelines
  • Mess timings and rules
  • Night study hours
  • Emergency procedures

24×7 Campus Security

Full-time campus security managed by P.R.D. personnel. CCTV cameras installed throughout the premises for round-the-clock monitoring.

Grievance Redressal System

A structured Grievance Redressal Mechanism allows students, parents, and staff to communicate directly with college management for quick resolution of concerns.
