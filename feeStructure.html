JEECUP Admission Process for Diploma Programs

Admission to GOVERNMENT POLYTECHNIC BAIJPUR BHITI is through JEECUP (Uttar Pradesh Joint Entrance Examination for Polytechnic).

JEECUP 2024 Key Information

  • Conducting Authority: Uttar Pradesh Board of Technical Education (BTEUP)
  • Exam Level: State Level
  • Courses Offered: Diploma in Engineering/Technology
  • Exam Mode: Computer Based Test (CBT)
  • Exam Duration: 3 Hours

Important Dates (Tentative)

Event Date
Application Start Date March 2024
Last Date to Apply April 2024
Admit Card Release May 2024
Exam Date June 2024
Result Declaration July 2024
Counselling Start August 2024

Eligibility Criteria

For Engineering/Technology Diploma
  • Passed 10th standard or equivalent examination
  • Minimum 35% marks (33% for SC/ST candidates)
  • Mathematics and Science as compulsory subjects
  • Age limit: No upper age limit
For Lateral Entry (2nd Year)
  • Passed 12th (PCM) or ITI (2 years) in relevant trade
  • Minimum 35% marks (33% for SC/ST candidates)
  • Age limit: No upper age limit

Documents Required for Admission

During Application Process
  • Scanned passport size photograph
  • Scanned signature
  • Class 10th mark sheet
  • Category certificate (if applicable)
During Counselling/Admission
  • JEECUP admit card
  • JEECUP rank card
  • Original Class 10th certificate
  • Transfer certificate
  • Character certificate
  • Domicile certificate
  • Category certificate (if applicable)
  • Income certificate (for fee waiver if applicable)
  • Passport size photographs (4 copies)

Note: Carry original documents along with 2 sets of photocopies for verification during counselling.
